About Us

At Trailer CAD, we are an Australian-based company specializing in precision-engineered semi-trailer designs for both industrial and custom applications. With expertise in 3D CAD modeling, manufacturing-ready blueprints, and structural analysis, we deliver high-quality, efficient, and cost-effective trailer solutions. Whether you need a heavy-duty transport trailer or a specialized custom design, we turn ideas into real-world, production-ready engineering solutions to meet your unique needs
